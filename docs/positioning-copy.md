## 7. What NOT to Say

| Avoid | Why | Instead |
|-------|-----|---------|
| "Revolutionary" | It's a utility tool | "Practical" or "useful" |
| "Enterprise-grade" | Overstates importance | "Works reliably" |
| "Changing the world" | DNS automation isn't | "My day job handles that" |
| "AI-powered" alone | Buzzwordy | "Built in a weekend" (shows speed) |
| "For nonprofits" | Not the primary audience | "Free forever, AGPL-licensed" |
